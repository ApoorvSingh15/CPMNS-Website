Mrs. Savita Singh

Mrs. Savita Singh, principal of Children's Palace, has been in the education line for all her life. She has made a significant contribution in the field of education. The sheer fact that teaching is not only her job but also her passion makes her unique and more effective in this field.

She has helped thousands of students to realize their true worth by guiding them in the right direction. She is the epitome of simplicity and leads a simple life. Maintaining the right balance between work and household is never easy but by her elegance and simplicity she makes it look easy.

As the principal of Children’s Palace, she has managed to build quite a repute and under her able guidance the school has won many awards. She has been featuring her school regularly in the Flower Show which is held at the governor’s house every year and has won many awards in the regard. The achievements of the school under her have been exceptional and she is an example of a true leader. The students passing out of the school go on to bag admissions in prestigious schools like La Marts, Lorreto Convent, CMS and many others. The education imparted at the school is of quality and the principal is vigilant that the schedule is being followed.

The students are advised to bring quality and healthy food so that they remain healthy. Various functions are organized by her so that children don’t face any lack of extra-curricular activities.

The work put in by her in the development of the school is unreal and the fact that the school is showing signs of pure brilliance shows that she is the right successor of former principal Mrs. Kaushalya Popli.
